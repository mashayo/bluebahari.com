Empowering women across Tanzania’s blue economy

From boat ownership and sustainable fishing to market access and ocean stewardship — we support women to lead and thrive.

Who We Are

Blue Bahari Group is a women-led initiative founded by Adv. Julieth Venant Rushuli. We champion women’s participation throughout the fisheries value chain — not only in processing and sales, but also in boat ownership and direct fishing.

Programs & Services

Processing & Value Addition

Hygienic handling, drying, packaging, and preservation.

Training & Capacity Building

Sustainable fishing, conservation, safety, business skills.

Community Trade Networks

Market linkages that connect women fishers with buyers.

Why It Matters

Income & Dignity

Women control more of the value chain and build resilient coastal livelihoods.

Sustainability

Communities protect reefs, mangroves, and fisheries for future generations.

Inclusive Growth

A stronger, fairer blue economy for Tanzania.
